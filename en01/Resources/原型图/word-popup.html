transforming

/trænsˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ v. 现在分词
熟悉
查询次数：3

本文中的含义

改变，转变

使某事物发生显著的变化或转变

Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming the way we live, work, and interact with one another.

其他含义

转化，转换

将某物从一种形式或性质转换为另一种

The company is transforming raw materials into finished products.

彻底改观

使某物的外观或特性发生显著变化

The renovation is transforming the old building into a modern office space.
